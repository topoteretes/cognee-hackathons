# ICP — Sponsor (DevTools Day Berlin 2027)

Who we sell sponsorship to for a **developer-first** conference in Berlin.

## The one-line ICP
A company that **sells tools to software developers** (frontend, backend, infra,
observability, API, databases) with a DevRel or developer-marketing motion and a
reason to be in front of an EU/DACH developer audience right now.

## Three sub-segments (all real in the data)
1. **Frontend / app platforms** — Vercel, Netlify, frameworks. The headline draw.
2. **Backend / data / infra** — Supabase, Sentry, Grafana, observability and databases.
3. **API / workflow tooling** — Postman and the API-first crowd.

## "Why now" triggers
- **Hiring DevRel or Field Marketing in EMEA / Berlin** (strongest signal).
- **Raised a round** or expanded EU developer programs in the last ~12 months.
- **Already sponsors developer events** and renews warm from Berlin 2026.

## Anti-ICP (see do-not-approach.csv)
- **Runs a competing developer conference** (WeAreDevelopers) → partner or
  cross-promote, do not pitch a sponsorship.
