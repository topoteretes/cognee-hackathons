# ICP — Speaker (GTM Tech Week London 2027)

Who we recruit to the stage. Speakers are **recruited, not sold** — the "deal" is a yes, not a cheque.
This is the filter behind `apollo_people-export_speakers.csv` and the speaker pipeline in
`attio_deals.csv`.

## The one-line ICP
A **recognised GTM voice** — founder, CRO, CMO, VP Sales/Marketing, RevOps/GTM-engineering leader, or
a sales/marketing creator — who actively talks about GTM, demand gen, RevOps or AI-in-GTM, and who
either is **UK/London-based** or has a **strong UK/EMEA draw**.

## Tiers (the `tier` column on each speaker)
- **A — keynote draws.** Names that sell tickets on their own. April Dunford, Sam Jacobs, Chris Walker,
  Dave Gerhardt, James Isilay, Adam Robinson, Daniel Disney, Kyle Coleman, Carles Reina, Frank Sondors,
  Marcus Sheridan. Pursue 1–2 as the headline keynote.
- **B — strong session speakers.** Heads/VPs and respected operators who fill a room and bring a real
  result. The backbone of the agenda.
- **C — practitioner / niche.** GTM engineers, Clay-crowd, specialist founders. Great for workshops and
  roundtables, lighter on top-of-funnel draw.

## "Why now" triggers (what `signals_feed.csv` watches, person-level via `person_linkedin_url`)
- **Posting actively on AI + GTM** — warm, will say yes to a stage.
- **Just shipped a notable GTM result** (scaled a number, ran a play worth telling).
- **Launched a book / podcast / community** — they want reach, the stage is reach.
- **London / UK based** or runs UK community (Pavilion London, Clay Club London, RevOps community).

## Bias for this edition
We are **light on UK/London keynote names** relative to the global field, so weight the search toward
UK-based A/B speakers (Cognism execs, Paddle, Pleo, GoCardless, ElevenLabs London, ScaleWise/Pavilion
London, the UK creator set: Daniel Disney, Tom Boston, Sophie Miller, Benjamin Dennehy).

## The #1 keynote question (the cross-cut demo)
Pick **one tier-1 keynote target** with: (a) genuine London draw, (b) an AI+GTM message that matches
"GTM is becoming an engineering discipline," and (c) a live "why now." The data supports a
defensible pick — and the warm path may run **through the anchor sponsor** (Clay's orbit), see
`account-deep-dive_ANCHOR.md`.

## Anti-ICP
- No-shows / purely promotional pitches (would turn the stage into "AI SDR spam" — the one thing the
  brand is against).
- Speakers whose employer is on `do-not-approach.csv` as a competing-event organiser, unless the talk
  is genuinely independent.
