# Distro Index

Distros are grouped by upstream family first. Put derivatives under the family that most directly shapes their package manager, release model, documentation, and troubleshooting path.

## Families

- [[arch/index]] - Arch Linux and Arch-based distributions
- [[debian/index]] - Debian, Ubuntu, and Debian-family distributions
- [[fedora/index]] - Fedora, RHEL-family, and rpm-ostree relatives
- [[opensuse/index]] - openSUSE and SUSE-family distributions
- [[nixos/index]] - NixOS and Nix-based systems
- [[independent/index]] - Independent distributions without a dominant upstream family
