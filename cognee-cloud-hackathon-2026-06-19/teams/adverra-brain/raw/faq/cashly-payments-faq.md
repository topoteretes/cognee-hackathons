# Cashly Payments FAQ

_Source: Cashly Help Center · Category: FAQ_

**Q: What payout methods does Cashly support?**
PayPal, bank transfer, gift cards, and cryptocurrency.

**Q: What is the minimum first withdrawal?**
It varies by region, from $5 to $20. After your first withdrawal there is no
minimum threshold.

**Q: Do I have to verify my identity?**
Yes, once — on your first withdrawal, via Veriff (selfie + ID document). Later
withdrawals do not require it.

**Q: What are the PayPal limits and fees?**
PayPal withdrawals range from $5 to $200 and carry a 5% fee.

**Q: How long do withdrawals take?**
PayPal, crypto, and gift cards are usually instant but can be delayed up to 30
minutes by random security checks. Bank transfer, Stash, and Revolut take up to
3 business days.

**Q: Can support speed up a pending withdrawal?**
No. Support cannot accelerate processing or give exact completion times.

**Q: I sent PayPal money to the wrong email — what now?**
Unclaimed payments auto-return to Cashly after 30 days and are then refunded to
your balance. If a stranger claims it first, it cannot be recovered.

**Q: Which countries can cash out to PayPal?**
Over 190 countries and territories.
