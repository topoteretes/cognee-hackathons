# Your First Withdrawal on Cashly: Requirements & Guide

_Category: Withdrawal / Issues · Source: Cashly Help Center · Last updated: 2026-05-28_

## Overview

Cashly is a rewarded-earnings platform where users complete tasks, surveys, and
partner offers to earn money and rewards. Payout options include gift cards,
PayPal, bank transfer, and cryptocurrencies.

## First Withdrawal Requirements

**ID Verification.** You will be prompted to verify your ID while making your
first withdrawal. Verification takes up to five minutes and requires camera
access for a selfie, as well as a physical identity document to confirm your
identity. Cashly uses a third-party verification provider (Veriff) for this step.

**Minimum Threshold.** The minimum withdrawal amount for your first cashout
varies by region, ranging from **$5 to $20**.

## Step-by-Step Withdrawal Process

1. **Access the Cashout page.** Select your account balance shown in the
   top-right corner (desktop) or at the top of the page (mobile).
2. **Choose a withdrawal method.** Pick from bank transfer, PayPal, gift cards,
   or cryptocurrency.
3. **Enter details.** Input your information and the amount you want to withdraw.
4. **Initiate withdrawal.** Press the green **Withdraw** button.
5. **Verify identity.** First-time users complete ID verification (selfie +
   document) through Veriff.

## Subsequent Withdrawals

After your first successful withdrawal the process is simpler. For withdrawals
after the first, **you do not need to verify your ID or meet a minimum
threshold** — you only need enough balance for your chosen method.
