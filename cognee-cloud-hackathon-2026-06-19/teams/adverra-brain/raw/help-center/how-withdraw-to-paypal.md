# How to Cash Out with PayPal on Cashly

_Category: Withdrawal / Cash · Source: Cashly Help Center · Last updated: 2026-05-28_

## Overview

There are two PayPal withdrawal flows depending on which payment processor
handled your transaction: **Tremendous** (current system) or **Tango** (legacy
system).

## PayPal via Tremendous (Primary Method)

1. **Access the Cashout page** — select your account balance in the top-right
   corner (desktop) or top of page (mobile).
2. **Choose PayPal** from the withdrawal options in the "Most Popular" section.
3. **Select an amount.** PayPal withdrawals are available from **$5 to $200**,
   with a **5% fee** applied to all transactions.
4. **Enter the email** linked to your PayPal account and confirm you understand
   the order is non-refundable.
5. **Click the green Withdraw button.** First-time users must complete ID
   verification with a government-issued document.
6. **Wait for the security hold to clear** — you'll receive a "Withdrawal Sent"
   notification.
7. **Check your email** for a message from the Tremendous reward system with a
   subject like "Redeem Cashly PayPal $X USD" and click the green **Redeem**
   button.
8. **Confirm your PayPal address** in the Tremendous portal to finalize.

## PayPal via Tango (Legacy Method)

Follow similar initial steps, but expect **two separate emails** — one
confirming the transaction and one from PayPal prompting you to claim funds by
logging into your account.
