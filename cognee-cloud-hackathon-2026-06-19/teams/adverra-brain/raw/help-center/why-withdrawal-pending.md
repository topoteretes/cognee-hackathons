# Why Is My Withdrawal Still Pending on Cashly?

_Category: Withdrawal / Issues · Source: Cashly Help Center · Last updated: 2026-05-28_

## Overview

Cashly withdrawal processing times depend on the payment method you selected.

## Processing Times

**Instant methods (with possible delays).** Most withdrawals via PayPal,
cryptocurrency, and gift cards are processed instantly. However, **random
security checks can extend processing by up to 30 minutes** to prevent fraud.

**Slower methods.** Bank transfers, Stash, and Revolut withdrawals require
manual processing and may take up to **3 business days**.

## Important Limitation

The Cashly support team **cannot accelerate** withdrawal processing or give an
exact completion time for a pending transfer.

## How to Check Status

- **Mobile:** Profile → Withdrawals → tap the arrow for details
- **Desktop:** Profile → Withdrawals tab

## Next Steps

If a withdrawal shows as **Rejected**, contact Cashly support. Otherwise, allow
pending transactions to process within the stated timeframes.
