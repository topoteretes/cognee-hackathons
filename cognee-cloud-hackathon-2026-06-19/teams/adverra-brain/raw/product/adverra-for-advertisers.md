# Adverra for Advertisers — Player Acquisition & Smart Ad Spend

_Source: Adverra internal product wiki · Owner: Product Team_

Adverra helps mobile game publishers and ad spenders acquire high-quality
players and spend smarter across the funnel.

## Value proposition

- **2–3x higher ROAS** versus typical UA channels.
- Access to **80M+ highly engaged users** through the Cashly platform.
- Support for **1,200+ games and brands**.
- Higher customer lifetime value (LTV) from reward-motivated, intent-rich users.

## What advertisers get

- **Data-driven campaigns** targeting high-quality users.
- **Global reach** with localized reward strategies.
- **Real-time analytics dashboards** for spend and funnel performance.
- **MMP integration** (mobile measurement partners) for clean attribution.
- **Dedicated account management** and ongoing optimization.

## 5-step launch framework

1. Initial consultation.
2. Campaign strategy.
3. Reward system design.
4. Data tracking / attribution integration.
5. Live deployment and optimization.
