# Cashly — Product Overview

_Source: Adverra internal product wiki · Owner: Product Team_

**Cashly** is Adverra's consumer-facing rewarded-earnings platform. Users
complete tasks, surveys, and partner game/app offers to earn real money and
rewards, which they can withdraw via PayPal, bank transfer, gift cards, or
cryptocurrency.

## How it fits Adverra's business

Adverra is a user-acquisition company for mobile game publishers and app
developers. Cashly is the demand-side engine: by rewarding highly engaged users
for trying partner games, Adverra delivers **high-quality installs and
2–3x higher ROAS** than typical ad networks. Cashly currently powers offers for
**1,200+ games and brands** and reaches **80M+ engaged users**.

## Core user loop

1. User signs up for Cashly (web app).
2. User browses the **Offerwall** of partner games and tasks.
3. User completes an offer (e.g., reach level 20 in a partner game).
4. Cashly credits coins; coins convert to a cash balance.
5. User withdraws via their preferred payout method.

## Key concepts

- **Offerwall** — the catalog of monetizable tasks and partner game offers.
- **Coins** — the in-app reward currency; converts to a USD cash balance.
- **Payout methods** — PayPal, bank transfer, gift cards, crypto.
- **ID verification** — required once, on the first withdrawal (via Veriff).
