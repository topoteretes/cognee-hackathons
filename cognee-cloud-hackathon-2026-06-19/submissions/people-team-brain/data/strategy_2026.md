# Source: strategy_2026.md — Honestel 2026 Strategy & Roadmap

Honestel is a mobile network operator whose market USPs are **transparent
pricing** and **fair practices** — no hidden fees, no surprise bills. These
customer promises drive what we must build in 2026.

## Flagship initiative: "No-Surprises" Transparent Billing Guarantee (launch Q3 2026)
We are publicly guaranteeing that customers never get a surprise bill. To
deliver this we must replace our **black-box third-party billing vendor** with
an **in-house real-time rating & billing engine** that can show charges as they
accrue. This is the single largest engineering initiative of the year.

Capability this requires:
- **Real-time streaming data engineering** (high-throughput event processing) —
  we do not currently have this in-house at scale.
- **Billing / payments domain expertise** (rating, invoicing, reconciliation).

## Secondary initiative: Fair Pricing recommender (H2 2026)
A system that proactively recommends the cheapest plan for each customer's usage
— reinforcing the "fair" USP. Requires a **Pricing Product Manager** and
**data science** capacity for the recommendation models.

## Customer satisfaction target
Raise CSAT to 4.6/5. This depends on scaling support operations in our existing
markets, not on new engineering hires.

## eSIM expansion
Launch eSIM in **2 new markets (Poland, Portugal)** in 2026 — gated on local
regulatory/compliance work, not net-new engineering capabilities.
