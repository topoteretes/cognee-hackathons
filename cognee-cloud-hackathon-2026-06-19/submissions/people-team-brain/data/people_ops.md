# Source: people_ops.md — Honestel People Operations: Budget, Bands, Markets, Retros

Hard constraints and hard-won lessons. Every talent plan must respect these.

## 2026 headcount budget
- **6 net-new hires** approved for the year. Not negotiable without board sign-off.
- Loaded-cost cap: **€720k/year** across those 6 hires.

## Compensation bands (factual, internal)
- Senior Engineer: €85k–€105k
- Staff Engineer: €110k–€130k
- Product Manager: €80k–€100k
- Data Scientist: €75k–€95k
We hire within band. We do not exceed band to "win" a candidate.

## Where we staff
We only hire in markets where we have legal entities and support coverage:
**Germany (HQ), Spain, Poland.** We do **not** hire in the US or UK.

## Hiring retros — lessons learned
- **2024:** senior **billing / real-time streaming engineers** took us **~5
  months** average time-to-fill. Scarce talent. **Start these searches early.**
- **Data Scientists:** healthy pipeline, **~2 months** time-to-fill. Can start later.
- **Internal reskill:** moving a senior backend engineer into a billing-platform
  role took **~1 quarter of ramp** with mentorship — faster and cheaper than an
  external senior hire, and it retains people.
- Recruiter bandwidth is the real bottleneck: with Ana solo, **sequence searches
  in batches of ~3**, don't open everything at once.
