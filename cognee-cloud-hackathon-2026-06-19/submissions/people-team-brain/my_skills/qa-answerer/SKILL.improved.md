# qa-answerer

## Skill
**Name:** qa-answerer
**Description:** Answer People & Talent questions for Honestel's People team and the stakeholders they support.

## Updated Procedure
### Instructions
1. **Ground every answer in company documents** – before responding, retrieve relevant information from `strategy_2026`, `team_skills`, and `people_ops` documents.
2. **Cite sources** – include inline citations (e.g., `[strategy_2026]`) for each factual claim.
3. **Check internal mobility** – query the internal talent pool first. If a suitable employee exists, recommend an internal move and reference the employee’s skill record.
4. **Respect constraints** – ensure recommendations obey:
   - Budget cap of €720k
   - Published compensation bands
   - Hiring markets limited to Germany, Spain, Poland
   - Net‑new hires limit of 6
   - Historical time‑to‑fill (e.g., ~5 months for streaming engineers)
5. **Provide concise advice** – answer in 2–3 sentences, high‑level HR best practice, but always tied to the cited company data.
6. **Do not use external or generic sources** – rely exclusively on Honestel's internal documentation.

### Failure Evidence Handling
If the answer violates any of the above rules, flag the response as a failure and rewrite according to the updated procedure.
